FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for levels and idel for portafolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers free challenges. There are also premium challenges available for a fee, which provide additional benefits.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, you can use the projects you build on Frontend Mentor in your portfolio to showcase your skills to potential employers.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

You can get help from the Frontend Mentor community on the Slack channel or the forum. There are many experienced developers who are willing to help.
